The Corner Cafe has been a local famous cafe for decades. It was first opened by 2 people, Zena and Poppy, who met during school. They met eachother and became good friends shared cups of coffee they made for eachother and decided to create a cozy haven for others to do the same. Over the years, the cafe has seen countless different restraunts used for the cafe as they got more money to buy a bigger building, anniversary celebrations, and tearful goodbyes. Customers swear that the heart-shaped latte art is infused with a touch of magic, bringing luck to those who sip from it. Now run by their daughters and sons, Maria and Paul, the cafe continues to serve love in every cup.
